DEPARTMENT of PHYSIOLOGY

Our Events


The Department of Physiology conducts regular scientific and social events for the benefit of department members and the Penn community at large. Our ongoing seminar series features distinguished scientists from throughout the globe while our newly launched “On the Road” series highlights ongoing work from Physiology faculty in our own back yard. Combine these talks with our robust mixture of journal clubs, Annual Departmental Retreat (and softball game), and you get a dynamic environment that is always exciting.

You can view our full calendar below. We are constantly making updates, so please check back frequently to see what is planned next!
