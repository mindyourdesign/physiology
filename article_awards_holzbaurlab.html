Holzbaur Lab Members Recognized!


March 2016

Congratulations to Jeff Nirschl, Eva Klinman, and Cory Simpson, MD, PhD!

The Holzbaur lab is in the spotlight as three members have earned awards for their research this year.

Jeff Nirschl and Eva Klinman, both MD/PhD candidates in the Holzbaur lab, have each been awarded the Dr. William F. Jeffers Prize for meritorious laboratory research in the field of neurology.

Cory Simpson, MD, PhD, a Dermatology fellow and postdoc in the Holzbaur lab, has just received two awards, one from the Dermatology Foundation and one from the La Roche-Posay Foundation.
