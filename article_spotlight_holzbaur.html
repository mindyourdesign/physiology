Erika Holzbaur Receives the Prestigious Javits Award!


June 2014

Erika Holzbaur, PhD

Congratulations to Erika Holzbaur, PhD, Professor of Physiology for receiving the Javits Award from the NIH! The National Advisory Neurological Disorders and Stroke (NANDS) Council selected Dr. Holzbaur for the Javits Neuroscience Investigator Award. The Congress of the United States suggested that special awards be made for research in the neurosciences in honor of the late Senator Jacob K. Javits.

These R37 MERIT AWARDS are given to investigators submitting research grant applications for competitive review who have a distinguished record of substantial contributions in neurological science and who can be expected to be highly productive over the next seven years of the award period.

Way to go Dr. Holzbaur!
