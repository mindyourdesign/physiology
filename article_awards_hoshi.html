Congratulations to Toshi Hoshi, Ph.D. for receiving the 2018 Jane M. Glick Graduate Student Teaching Award!


March 2018

Toshinori Hoshi, PhD

Congratulations to Toshi Hoshi, Ph.D. for being chosen as the recipient of the 2018 Jane Glick Graduate Student Teaching Award!

This award, which recognizes an outstanding faculty member for training, mentoring and educating the next generation of biomedical scientists, is well-deserved and long overdue. Toshi has had a long-standing and exemplary commitment to teaching in the BGS and PSOM, with consistently rave reviews from students and indeed other faculty for the clarity of his lectures and his commitment to the educational mission.

Toshi will receive the award at the BGS Graduation Ceremony on May 14, 2018 in the Smilow Center for Translational Research. The reception begins at 12:30pm, and the ceremony is scheduled from 1:30pm to 2:30pm.

Please join us in congratulating Toshi!
