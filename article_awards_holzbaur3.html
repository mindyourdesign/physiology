Erika Holzbaur, Ph.D. Receives the 2016 Jane M. Glick Graduate Student Teaching Award


March 2016

Erika Holzbaur, PhD

BGS is delighted to announce that this year’s recipient of the Jane M. Glick Graduate Student Teaching Award is Erika Holzbaur, PhD, Professor of Physiology.

Dr. Holzbaur has provided long-standing, exemplary service to the BGS/PSOM/Penn core mission of educating, mentoring and training the next generation of biomedical scientists. Erika received considerable praise and appreciation from her colleagues and students for her teaching and mentoring acumen, as well as for her leadership roles in the CAMB across many years. Her dedication to these efforts exemplifies the type of scientist/educator represented by Jane Glick.

Erika will be presented with this award during the BGS Graduation Ceremony, scheduled for May 16, 2016 in the BRB Glen Gaulton Auditorium. Please join us in congratulating Erika on receiving this award!

Congratulations Erika!
