Erika Holzbaur, Ph.D. Receives the 2015 Stanley N. Cohen Biomedical Research Award


October 2015

Erika Holzbaur, PhD

Congratulations to Erika Holzbaur, Ph.D., Professor of Physiology for receiving the 2015 Stanley N. Cohen Biomedical Research Award!

The Stanley N. Cohen Biomedical Research Award is given annually to a researcher in the Perelman School of Medicine, in recognition of their contributions during the previous year in the broad field of biomedical research. Penn Medicine will honor Erika at the annual Awards of Excellence Banquet in November.

Previous winners in the Department of Physiology of this prestigious award include Yale E. Goldman, MD, PhD (2005), H. Lee Sweeney, Ph.D. (2010) and J. Kevin Foskett, Ph.D. (2013).

Congratulations Erika!
