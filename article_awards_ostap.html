Congratulations to Mike Ostap, Ph.D. for receiving the MERIT AWARD from the NIGMS!


June 2016

E. Michael Ostap, PhD

Congratulations to Michael Ostap, Ph.D. for being awarded the prestigious MERIT AWARD from the National Institute of General Medicine Sciences (NIGMS)!

This award represents recognition by the NIH of Mike's continuous high level contributions to science over many years. In practical terms, it means that instead of a normal 4-year R01, his grant renewal will be awarded for 5 years, with a high probability of being extended for 5 years beyond that.

Receiving this award is a terrific honor and great achievement. Please join us in congratulating Mike!
