Ben Prosser Chosen as the 2017 Recipient of the Linda Pechenik Montague Investigator Award!


January 2017

Benjamin Prosser, PhD

Congratulations to Ben Prosser, Assistant Professor of Physiology, for being chosen as the 2017 recipient of the Linda Pechenik Montague Investigator Award!

This award was created 5 years ago by philanthropist Linda Pechenik Montague to support the work of the most promising research faculty at the Perelman School of Medicine. Receiving such an award would be an honor in itself, but there is also the tangible benefit that it provides a total of $100,000 for Ben to spend to advance his research program. We expect that he will have no trouble using it imaginatively, advised, no doubt, by his lab members.

Please join us in congratulating Ben for this achievement!
