Teji Khurana taking Human Physiology to New Heights!


January 15, 2016

Tejvir Khurana, MD, PhD

Congraulations to Teji Khurana for taking his course "Human Physiology", CAMB 532, to new heights of student application.

Human Physiology is a graduate level course in physiology that is taken by students from several graduate groups, including CAMB, Pharmacology, Neuroscience and Bioengineering.

Because the course is mandatory for some students, and it is primarily a didactic presentation, it has been difficult for it to achieve the highest student ratings. Neverthless, under Teji’s new leadership, the course broke through the critical 3.0 ranking (out of 4) to a 3.1!

We all benefit from Teji's achievement, as the graduate group continues to set a standard of excellence in higher education and beyond.
